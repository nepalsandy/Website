About Us

Flying Pegasus Travels and tours cordially welcomes you, we believe www.flyingpegasus.com will become a travel shop for your holiday arrangement. Our Tour programs are designed to bring you into close contact with an unspoiled, beautiful land, Culture, tradition, religion and diversity, charming citizens, magnificent mountains, and stately architecture. We Specialized to arrange Corporate leisure Tours, Adventure Tours, Culture Tours, nature tours Such as Trekking, Mountaineering, Rafting, Wildlife adventure, Booking(Cruise, Flight, Train, Bus), Hotel Reservation and special interest Tour for sophisticated travelers by our adequately qualified team. We contribute from every tour and booking some percentage to support the local community, Local NGO, Health Concern, School, educate poor Kids and Helpless, homeless orphanage Kids. Our clients are encouraged Travel & Contribution, our aim is not only to provide a travel service but also to create awareness in the local community in relation to environmental issues, Health Issue, Education issues to local peoples and visitors.
