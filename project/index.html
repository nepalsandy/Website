About Us

Flying Pegasus Travels and tours cordially welcomes you, we believe www.flyingpegasus.com will become a travel shop for your holiday arrangement. Our Tour programs are designed to bring you into close contact with an unspoiled, beautiful land, Culture, tradition, religion and diversity, charming citizens, magnificent mountains, and stately architecture. We Specialized to arrange Corporate leisure Tours, Adventure Tours, Culture Tours, nature tours Such as Trekking, Mountaineering, Rafting, Wildlife adventure, Booking(Cruise, Flight, Train, Bus), Hotel Reservation and special interest Tour for sophisticated travelers by our adequately qualified team. We contribute from every tour and booking some percentage to support the local community, Local NGO, Health Concern, School, educate poor Kids and Helpless, homeless orphanage Kids. Our clients are encouraged Travel & Contribution, our aim is not only to provide a travel service but also to create awareness in the local community in relation to environmental issues, Health Issue, Education issues to local peoples and visitors. Our partners include Major Hotels, Resorts, Airline, Cruise Line with a huge worldwide network, connection some are agencies, individual in different countries Australia, New Zealand, United State of America, United Kingdom, Germany, Finland, France, Spain, Norway, Holland, Belgium, Switzerland, Thailand, Russia, Denmark, Sweden, Tibet/China, Sweden, Hong Kong, India, Singapore, Bhutan, Magnolias, Bali, Ireland, Malaysia, Mexico, Brazil, Taiwan, Japan, South Korea, Greece, Turkey, and Luxemburg. You can find us most of Guide Books & Lonely Planet about us. For your logistic support, we provide you high-quality equipment, High-Quality Service by loyal friendly Professional staff respecting clients desire, opinion & wish. Our team members are always mindful, helpful, friendly, attentive to your needs, and always there for your service and reach your ultimate goal. To help you prepare your trips, we have put together detailed background information on each individual trip with maps, itineraries, altitude, graphs, photos, and cost details. We are always at your service and waiting to serve you smiling with taking great care. We heartily welcome yours advice, comments in every aspect, that will help us further to improve our more quality service. Our principle & belief Client is our GOD!! Client Satisfaction is our MOTTO!!
